"Anna Kyrii Architectural and Design Group" company was founded in 2008.

We work as a General Designer and develop for our clients all sections of project documentation without any exception.

The company specializes in development of the following types of design works:

- Consulting on any issues related to the design process of the development of architectural objects

- Town-planning calculations with the definition of boundary building parameters and possible risks

- Project documentation: Pre-project, Schematic Design, Project, Work Project and Working Documentation

- Construction design supervision

The number of projects developed by our company includes above 250 projects in the following categories: public buildings and constructions, residential multi-apartment buildings, office buildings and business centers, multifunctional complexes, medical institutions, schools, kindergartens, leisure centers, cottage towns, interiors of public spaces.
